About Us

The Manoj Sancheti Memorial Trust (MSMT) was established in memory of Shri Manoj Sancheti, whose life was marked by compassion and dedication to the welfare of all living beings.

We focus on cow welfare, community welfare, and social justice, collaborating with various organizations to amplify our impact.

Registered in Bengaluru, Karnataka, with Darpan ID: KA/2024/0430668 and Income Tax PAN: AAJTM4137B.

What We Do

Cow Welfare

We provide care and resources for cows in need.

Community Welfare

We organize blood donation drives and provide relief to poor communities.

Social Justice

We advocate for social justice and provide resources for marginalized communities.

Why We Do It

Our mission is to honor the legacy of Shri Manoj Sancheti by spreading compassion and justice through our activities, helping those in need.

Impact on Society

Through our efforts, we have organized numerous blood drives, assisted underprivileged communities, and supported animal welfare programs, creating lasting impacts on society.
